Biography

Elizabeth Serrano

Hi, my name is Elizabeth Serrano. I currently attend Kennesaw State University (2024), where I am pursuing a major in Computer Science with a minor in Software Engineering. Before transferring to Kennesaw State, I spent two years at Georgia Southern University, where I was involved in student life. During my time there, I served as a public representative for the Latin Club, helping bring awareness to the organization. At KSU, I continue to expand my academic and professional interests by participating in organizations such as the Society of Women Engineers. I plan to graduate in June 2026 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
